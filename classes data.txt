life recovered after each encounter:10%

glossery:
stage - a series of 10 encounters
encounter - a 1 on 1 fight against an enemy, each attacking by its attack or spell speed
level: each point put into a class is considered a level in that class
abilities: attacks or spells that can be leveled up by using them more often and gain attunement each time they level up
strength: +1 life per strength, 1% increased melee damage per 10 strength
dexterity: 2% increased defenses per 10 dexterity, 2% increased critical hit chance per 10 dexterity
intelligence: +1 mana per intelligence, 1% increased spell damage per 10 intelligence
defenses:
armor: full effectiveness against physical damage, 50% against elemental damage, 25% against chaos damage, the amount of damage reduction against a hit provided by armor cannot exceed 90%
evasion rating: evading a hit prevents all damage and effects that would result from that hit, the percentage chance to evade depends on the defenders evasion rating vs the attackers accuracy rating, evasion has full effectivess against attacks and 50% against spells, the maximum chance to evade a hit is 90%
energy shield: acts as an additional hit pool, protecting a defenders life until it is depleted, 25% of your energy shield is recovered after each encounter during a stage, chaos damage bypasses 50% of energy shield by default
resistances: mitigate damage from non-physical damage types, the default maximum resistance is 75% and cannot exceed 90%
block: blocking a hit reduces its damage by a percentage equal to the defenders block power, 50% by default, maximum chance to block hits is 75%
dodge: dodging a hit prevents all damage and effects that would result from that hit, the maximum chance to dodge is 75% and cannot exceed 90%, chance to ddoge is not influenced by an attackers accuracy rating
damage: damage generally occurs either from hits or as damage over time from ailements, any quantity of damage belongs to one of five damage types:
physical,lightning,cold,fire or chaos, a single hit can deal damage of multiple types at once. lightning, cold and fire damage are further classfied as elemental damage
ailements: status effects which can be eapplied through hits, if a hit has a chance to inflict a specific ailment and also deals damage of the required type(s). the different ailments are divided into two categories: damaging and non-damaging ailments.
damaging ailments: bleeding, ignite and poison, these ailments deal damage over time of a specific type, with their base damage determined by the final pre-mitigation damage dealt of the required type(s) by the inflicting hit.
bleed: 
    base damage per second: 30% of physical hit damage
    damage type:physical
    duration: 2 seconds
    ailement behavior: the strongest stack of bleeding deals full damage, while additional active stacks deal 50% of their damage
ignite:
    base damage per second: 45% of fire hit damage
    damage type: fire
    duration: 2 seconds
    ailment behavior: the strongest stack of ignite deals full damage, while additional active stacks deal 25% of their damage
poison:
    base damage per second: 15% of combined physical and chaos hit damage
    damage type:chaos
    duration: 3 seconds
    ailment behavior: all active poison stacks deal their full damage
non-damaging ailments: shock and chill, these ailments cause debuffs based on their combined effect across all active stacks, with the effect of a single stack determined by the final post-mitigation damage dealt of the required type(s) by the inflicting hit. the combined effected of a non-damaging ailment across all stacks is limited to an amount depending on the type of ailment.
shock:
    required damage type: lightning
    debuff effect: 1% increased damage taken per 1% of shock
    duration: 3 seconds
    maximum effect: 50%
chill:
    required damage type: cold
    debuff effect: 1% less speed per 1% effect of chill
    duration: 3 seconds
    maximum effect: 35%
critical hits: a critical hit multiplies its damage by its critical damage multiplier, modifiers such as 'increased critical hit chance' multiply your base critical hit chance gained from weapons,spells or other sources
classes data:
maxPointsPerUpgrade:5
each upgrade can be leveled up 5 times for example if a sorcerer puts 5 points in increasedLife, the total bonus would be 4% * 5 = 20%
the classBonus is only activated when the amount of points used reaches the required amount, for example to get the classBonus as a sorcerer, 10 points should be put in upgrades
